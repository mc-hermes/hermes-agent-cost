Add-on: Second Brain

Hermes Agent gives you an assistant. Second Brain gives it a memory of your entire business.

Forward an email → it gets processed, linked to existing knowledge, and stored in your private knowledge base. Forward a meeting transcript, a client note, an invoice — same flow. No tagging. No manual entry. The system enriches itself while you sleep.

Ask "What did Client X ask for in January?" or "Show me every conversation about the pricing proposal" — and get the answer in seconds, with sources.
