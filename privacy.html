Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

Maloha Coast Sdn Bhd ("we", "us", "our") operates a Discord application ("the Bot") that provides AI-powered assistance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

When you interact with the Bot, we may collect the following:

DataPurpose
Discord User IDTo identify users, track usage limits, and personalise responses.
Discord Server IDTo associate the Bot with the correct server and its configuration.
Channel IDTo route responses to the correct conversation.
Message ContentTo generate AI responses. Messages directed at the Bot (mentions, commands) are processed in real time.
Command & Usage LogsTo monitor performance, debug issues, and improve the service.

3. How We Use Data

  • Service Delivery — Processing your messages to generate AI responses is the core function of the Bot.
  • Improvement — Aggregated, anonymised usage patterns help us improve the Bot's performance and reliability.
  • Security & Abuse Prevention — We monitor for misuse, spam, and violations of our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build user profiles for commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored on secure servers and retained only as long as necessary:

  • Message content — Processed in real time; not stored beyond the session unless required for debugging.
  • User & Server IDs — Retained while the Bot is active on your server.
  • Usage logs — Retained for up to 90 days for operational purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

The Bot uses AI model providers (such as DeepSeek) to generate responses. Your messages and Discord IDs may be transmitted to these providers solely for the purpose of generating a response. We select providers that do not use API-submitted data for model training. You can request details about our current providers at any time.

We do not share your data with any other third parties except as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent by removing the Bot from your server.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@malohacoast.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via our Discord server or other channels. Continued use after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: info@malohacoast.com
