The Solution

What exactly is a gbrain?

Think of it as a second hard drive for your professional knowledge — that an AI can read and write to.

You dump in everything that matters: your career frameworks, your best LinkedIn posts, workshop transcripts, client notes, common questions and your answers to them, even your signature phrases and tone of voice. Over time, the system learns what you know and how you think. Then when you need a LinkedIn draft, a workshop slide deck, or a personalized follow-up email for a lead, the AI drafts it in your voice, using your frameworks, referencing your real client examples — not generic ChatGPT copy.

It's private. It's yours. And it gets smarter the more you use it.

Lead Management

Auto-qualify & reply to every registrant
AI reads every Google Form submission, applies your eligibility rules (5+ yrs experience, not students), tags each lead by career stage + blocker, and drafts the approve or polite-decline message.

Conversion & Follow-Up

05 / Personalised Post-Workshop Follow-Up
Every attendee gets a tailored nudge
AI drafts individualised outreach referencing each attendee's stated blocker and clarity level, nudging them toward DYC. You review and send — 30 messages in minutes, not hours.
