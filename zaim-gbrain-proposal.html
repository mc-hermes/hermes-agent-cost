The Solution

What exactly is a gbrain?

Think of it as a thinking partner that actually knows your material.

You dump in everything that matters: your career frameworks, your best LinkedIn posts, workshop transcripts, client notes, common questions and your answers to them. Over time, the system understands what you know and how you think. Then when you sit down to create, it surfaces angles you haven't considered, generates hooks, pulls up relevant case studies, and suggests frameworks to apply — so you're never starting from zero. You do the writing. It does the thinking prep.

It's private. It's yours. And it gets smarter the more you feed it.

9 ways it fuels your creative process

Content & Top-of-Funnel

01 / Idea Engine
Never stare at a blank page again
Your DYL methodology, career clarity exercises, and 50+ best posts stored as a searchable knowledge base. When you sit down to write, the AI surfaces angles, hooks, and talking points drawn from your own frameworks — so you start with a menu of ideas, not an empty cursor.

02 / Content Angle Extractor
One workshop → a dozen fresh angles
Workshop recordings and transcripts fed in. AI identifies the most provocative moments, counterintuitive insights, and re-frame-worthy stories — giving you a list of angles to develop into posts. You pick. You write.

Lead Management

03 / Lead Screening Assistant
Auto-qualify & reply to every registrant
AI reads every Google Form submission, applies your eligibility rules (5+ yrs experience, not students), tags each lead by career stage + blocker, and drafts the approve or polite-decline message.
Cuts screening from 2 hrs to 10 min per workshop
04 / Workshop Angle Generator
Talking points tailored to who's actually in the room
Pulls from your framework library + the latest registrant segmentation. If 70% of this month's registrants say "career change," the AI suggests which DYL exercises to emphasise and which stories to tell — so every workshop feels custom-built for that specific audience.
Prep time: 2 hrs → 15 min

Conversion & Follow-Up

05 / Personalised Follow-Up Prompter
Every attendee gets a relevant next step
AI suggests personalised follow-up angles for each attendee based on their stated blocker and clarity level — which DYL exercise to recommend, which past post to share, which objection to address. You add your voice. 30 messages in minutes.
Higher conversion, still authentically you
06 / Objection Handler
Instant answers for common DMs and emails
FAQs about pricing, time commitment, "is this right for me?" — AI drafts replies from your stored answers and past conversations. You review and send.
Inbox triage: 30 min → 5 min daily

Programme Delivery

07 / Pre-Session Thinking Prompts
Walk into every coaching call with fresh questions
Before each DYC session, AI generates thinking prompts from that client's stored profile: patterns in their blockers, connections to similar client journeys, questions worth exploring. You decide what to ask. You run the session.
Prep per session: 20 min → 2 min
08 / B2B Pitch Idea Generator
Corporate proposals that write themselves from your case studies
AI suggests pitch angles for corporate training by pulling relevant case studies, testimonials, and methodology from your knowledge base — then assembles a proposal skeleton you can polish and personalise.
Proposal creation: 3 hrs → 20 min
